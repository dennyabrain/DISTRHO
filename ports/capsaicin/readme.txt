Information-------------------------------------------------------------

Capsaicin is a powerful multipart polyphonic vector additive subtractive
with effects synthesizer based on Paul Nasca's incredible ZynAddSubFx
synthesizer. All parts of the synth can be automated to produce evolving
sounds and it is specially suitable for live usage with its gorgeous interface.


Features --------------------------------------------------------------

- 16 part multitimbral
- selectable monophonic and polyphonic
- 4 additive oscillators per voice
- up to 128 partials per oscillator
- 4 stage amplitude envelope
- 6 stage tone envelope
- react to velocity, pitchbend, aftertouch and midi CC
- 1 filter per voice (4 filter types)
- 1 lfo / 1 envelope on the filter
- 4 effects stages (chorus - drive - eq - delay)
- X-Y joystick vector mixer
- selectable midi keyboard
- part edit page
- global mixer page
- preset selector
- output resampling
